## **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** 

## **Product Name** 

## **CreatorTrust** 

**The Trust Layer for Influencer Marketing** 

## **1. Vision** 

Build the world's most trusted platform for influencer-brand collaborations by combining marketplace dynamics with a reputation and AI matching engine. 

Unlike existing influencer platforms that focus primarily on discovery, CreatorTrust enables brands and creators to confidently collaborate through transparency, reviews, AI recommendations, and verified reputation. 

## **2. Problem Statement** 

## **Brand Challenges** 

- Finding genuine creators. 

- Detecting fake engagement. 

- Unpredictable campaign execution. 

- No visibility into creator reliability. 

- Manual outreach and negotiation. 

## **Influencer Challenges** 

- Delayed or missing payments. 

- Unprofessional brands. 

- Difficulty finding quality campaigns. 

- No reputation system to showcase professionalism. 

- Constant underpricing.
